Goals

Cossiplay is a small family-run local business operating since 2012. They create costumes designed for cosplay and organze cosplay parties every month. The website designed for Cossiplay contains all of the information necessary to participate in the parties and to buy the costumes.

Success Evaluation

The site's success will not only be evaluated with the hits count but also with the number of people registering in the "Sign-Up" page. If they register, it means that the client has found what he wanted, and found the website attractive.

Target Audience

The website is designed for everyone interested in the world of cosplay. The target age group goes from 18 until 50 years old.
